Welcome to the home page of Project Dissonance! Project Dissonance is a Metroidvania RPG in development by SleepySage. Along the way, Shan will find cool stuff that lets him find more cool stuff, uncover secrets, and meet people-- heroes, villains, and weirdos alike.
