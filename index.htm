About
Welcome to the home page of Project Dissonance! Project Dissonance is a Metroidvania RPG in development by SleepySage. The main plot of Project Dissonance revolves around an admittedly average person named Shan, who is tasked with investigating a paranormal phenomenon around his world: everyone he knows is receiving magical powers out of nowhere!? Along the way, Shan will find cool stuff that lets him find more cool stuff, uncover secrets, and meet people-- heroes, villains, and weirdos alike. Prepare to break outside of what you thought you knew… This site is under construction and will be subject to change. If you’re here from Fangame Marathon, then welcome! I appreciate you stopping by to see my work and what is to become of the game you’ve seen. The game’s an early work-in-progress, but I would like to present a playable demo soon, so please do stick around for that. It’s gonna be sweet.
