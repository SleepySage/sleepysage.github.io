About
So, what is this “Dissonance” game? In short, this game is a metroidvania RPG hybrid about Shan, an ordinary guy, who’s just living his life when suddenly, everyone’s getting cool magical powers. His adventure to find out what exactly is going on, brings him outside the reality he thought he knew, and into a brave new frontier outside of it. When does it come out? It’ll be a long while, maybe sometime in 2020. However, I will have periodic Demo releases available to some members of my Discord server, and… later in development… an exclusively private Steam prerelease testing group. What will I be able to play it on? Basically all of the demos will be on Windows, however I do have the ability to port it to Mac OS X and Linux. And, if I can manage to get the money and legal know-how, we might even be able to see it on the Nintendo Switch…! For real? You can port it to the Switch? The Switch export module costs some big-ones but yes we can totally do that if I get some mad cash and some time to make sure nothing breaks on our new console. I might invest in your game. What will it cost on Steam? Probably 6 bucks, which is actually cheaper than most high-shelf metroidvania titles. Do you have any way to communicate with fans? I shill my game in some Discord servers, sometimes post about it in Twitter, but you can find info to contact me on the “About” page. What all goes into developing this? Most of the time I’m passing ideas among my team, making art assets, coding, or just slacking off (game dev isn’t easy, lol). I stream on Twitch.tv making sprites every so often, but you can ask me anything in my Discord server about what goes into it. I really like some of your character designs. Do you accept fanart?
