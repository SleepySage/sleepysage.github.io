Here, you can find info on some of the characters you may   meet in Project Dissonance. Shan Just a chill, yet very ordinary dude. Spends most of his time watching TV or playing video games. Also, slightly introverted in nature. He’s always looking for new people to hang out with, and is generally nice to his peers. His call to action involves an unreal phenomenon that bestows people he knows including himself with magical powers leading him to go investigate what exactly is happening in the world he knows… Diane She is a very kindhearted person. Always looking out for her friends, and is very generous. However, she is a bit of a klutz, and her generosity has led to her getting hurt. As a result, she doesn’t socialize as much as she used to. She joins Shan in his adventure to solve why this magical phenomenon is occurring. This is just a hunch, but I think she might like Shan. Cody A cool dude. Seriously. This dude is all about being cool, dude. There is no dude who keeps cool in stressful situations like Cody. Hobbies include skateboarding, chewing gum, and doing sick kickflips. On the personality side of things, he’s a very simple-minded guy: don’t mess with him and he won’t mess with you. Not exactly the most educated of people, but he does get along with similar-minded dudes (which just happen to be skaters). Amber A pyrokinetic lass, who discovered her fire-manipulating powers when the world was blessed with magic. She is a mostly generous girl who can also definitely backsass you if you say something really stupid. She may look young, but she is just short. Some people call her a “shortie”… -- the same people got a deserved burn from her fireball abilities. You might not want to heckle with her! Bentley The quiet type. No one knows exactly why he won’t speak, but he’s proficient at displaying emotion without making a sound. In fact, there’s still much information we don’t know about him. What does he like? What are his hobbies? If there’s one thing we know for sure, it is that he isn’t a bad fellow. If you can somehow get to know him, I’m sure you can see it for yourself. Rosa The professional flirt. She comes from the deep dark underworld and has mastered the technique of flirting and subsequently seducing whoever she deems fit -- boys, girls, and what-have-you. She’ll date them. Of course, this usually results in short-lived relationships between her and her “subject”. However, she makes sure to thoroughly satisfy her partner before departing for the next one. Travis He was found in a trashcan somewhere that he made into his home. He lives the hobo life, is kind of oblivious, but generally a nice guy. He came armed with a slightly-foul-smelling revolver gun that is unloaded. Some say, if he can get just one bullet for that revolver, he can unleash its ultimate power. We don’t know how true that is, but you could try it… what could possibly happen? Also, he has a cool hat too. That’s just a bonus. Flauren A wonder of nature -- A human/plant hybrid! How did this come to be? Some time ago, her former self, “Lauren”, eventually lost hope for herself and society, and made the unfortunate decision to attempt suicide. She loves the forest and nature, so she spent her last hours with the forest before getting partially eaten by a plant. She ended up symbiotically fusing with it instead…! Now she is a one-of-a-kind plant-human. She is mostly introverted and shy, but inside, she is mostly content with becoming what she loved… To be one with nature. Paris What a rich kid. What a posh kid. There isn’t much personality here, it was overtaken by capitalist greed and looking fabulous. One codeword that could describe him is “posh”, or “rich”. Did we mention that he is very rich? And no, he won’t hand off some of that cash to buy upgrades or anything. That’d be stupid. He’s probably not great at parties, either. Queen Bethany This island used to be a monarchy with this lady and her current ex as the top authority figures running it… But, that was put to an end when the king left, and the people wanted to have a democracy instead. Now Bethany is more or less just a simple President, rather than being in full control. She gets to keep her nice dress though. She makes the active effort to be a caring and responsible authority around the place, and the people are content with her being in charge of the place. She carries with her an elegant and serene aura that cannot be replaced.
